# Product Requirements Document (PRD): HiReady

## 1. Project Overview

HiReady is an AI-powered interview simulation platform designed for Indonesian students and fresh graduates (ages 20–26). The application provides a safe, realistic environment to practice interviews using a real-time, 3D animated AI Avatar that acts as an interviewer.

**Core Vision:** To be a "practice mirror" that provides objective, constructive feedback to help candidates grow, rather than a judging platform.

---

## 2. Target Audience

- **Primary:** University students and fresh graduates in Indonesia.
- **Tone:** Professional yet approachable, modern, secure, and encouraging.
- **Language:** Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia).

---

## 3. Product Features & User Flow

### 3.1 Authentication (Screen 1)

- Clean Login/Register interface.
- Tabbed navigation between "Daftar" (Sign Up) and "Masuk" (Log In).
- Fields: Full Name, Email, Password.
- Social Login: "Lanjutkan dengan Google".

### 3.2 Onboarding (Screens 2–6)

A one-time, 4-step guided process to personalize the user experience:

1. **Welcome:** Introduction to the AI simulation.
2. **Path Selection:** Choose between "Swasta / Korporat" (Corporate/BUMN) or "Kedinasan" (Civil Service/Military).
3. **Target Selection:** Choose specific companies (e.g., Gojek, Tokopedia, BCA) or institutions (e.g., Akmil, Akpol, STAN).
4. **Position Selection:** Choose specific roles (e.g., Product Manager, Software Engineer, Taruna).

### 3.3 Dashboard (Screen 7)

- Personal greeting and performance summary.
- **"Dari sesi terakhirmu" Section:** Highlights the 3 dimensions most in need of attention.
- **Primary Call to Action:** Large, prominent "Mulai Sesi Wawancara" button.
- Exercise history list with dates, targets, and status.

### 3.4 Pre-Session Disclaimer (Screen 8)

- Technical checklist: Headset usage, camera activation, stable connection, and quiet environment.
- Confirmation checkbox required to activate the "Saya siap" (I'm ready) button.

### 3.5 Interview Session (Screen 9)

- **Immersive View:** 3D Avatar interviewer dominates the screen.
- **Self-View:** Small PiP (Picture-in-Picture) window for the user.
- **Real-time Transcript:** Floating panel on the right showing the conversation history and AI word detection.
- **Liquid Glass Controls:** Minimalist floating bar for Mute, Camera, and End Session.

### 3.6 Interview Results (Screen 10)

- **Focus Mode:** No sidebar or top bar to ensure full attention on feedback.
- **8 Dimensions of Communication:** Independent evaluation of Tutur Kata, Intonasi, Kecepatan Bicara, Filler Words, Pemilihan Kalimat, Kelengkapan Jawaban, Konsistensi, and Kepercayaan Diri.
- **Scale:** Three levels (Perlu Latihan, Berkembang, Mahir) using soft colors (Orange, Blue, Green).
- **Question Analysis:** Breakdown of every question with specific feedback (✓ for good, △ for improvement).
- **Filler Word Analytics:** Visual bar chart showing the frequency of specific filler words.

### 3.7 Progress & History (Screen 11)

- **Riwayat Sesi:** List of past sessions.
- **Progress Tab:** Interactive spline chart showing development trends across communication dimensions over time.

---

## 4. Visual Identity (Executive Maroon)

- **Primary Color:** Deep Maroon (#800000) for a professional and authoritative feel.
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (e.g., Plus Jakarta Sans).
- **Style:** Clean, minimalist, with premium "Liquid Glass" (glassmorphism) elements in the simulation view.
- **Navigation:** Fixed sidebar for main dashboard navigation; centered focus for reports.
